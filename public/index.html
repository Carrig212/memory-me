About Memory/Me

Last updated 10/11/2017 by Aaron McAdams

Memory/Me is an application that strives to help people organise and track their daily lives. Offering both an easy to use Calendar with customisable events, and a daily journal (in the form of a rich text editor) to keep track of your life in an easy manner. Sign-up using an existing google account or your own personal email and everything you enter will be saved when you return.

Updates/Planned features

Last updated 10/11/2017 by Aaron McAdams

While we are happy with what Memory/Me offers at present, we are striving to update and improve this application. New features will hopefully include a mobile edition of the application (for android and iOS), a daily live calendar that will be able to notify you of events when you need it and task estimation functions that will give you feedback when you schedule tasks/assignments with specific due dates. We will ofcourse keep you updated on the progress of each of these.
